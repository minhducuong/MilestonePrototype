OUR VISION

Our mission is to support the Closing the Gap initiative by focusing on Outcome 1: Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people enjoy long and healthy lives and Outcome 5: Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students achieve their full learning potential. We raise awareness of critical issues like reduced life expectancy, infant mortality, and low school attendance. We provide culturally relevant resources—such as health tips in Indigenous languages and maps of ACCHS services—and offer support for early learning and family engagement. By tracking progress through clear data on health and education, we strive to help close the gap and create lasting impact.
