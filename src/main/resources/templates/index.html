Overview

This website aims to shed light on key disparities faced by Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities by focusing on life expectancy (Outcome 1) and early childhood education (Outcome 5). Through visual comparisons of population data from 2016 to 2021, we highlight progress and remaining gaps in health and education.
